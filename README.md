# Bike_Sales_Dashboard

## Project Overview
The **Bike Sales Dashboard** analyzes customer demographics and purchasing behavior to understand factors influencing bike purchases. This dashboard helps businesses identify target customers and optimize sales strategies.

---

## Business Objectives
- Analyze income patterns by gender
- Study customer age distribution and purchase behavior
- Understand commuting distance impact on bike purchases
- Segment customers by region, education, and marital status

---

## Dashboard Components
### Average Income per Purchase
- Bar chart comparing **male vs female income**
- Purchase decision categorized as **Yes / No**

### Age Status of Customers
- Line chart showing purchase trends across:
  - Adolescent
  - Middle Age
  - Old

### Customer Commute Distance
- Line chart analyzing bike purchase likelihood based on commute distance:
  - 0–1 miles
  - 1–2 miles
  - 2–5 miles
  - 5–10 miles
  - 10+ miles

---

## Interactive Filters
- Marital Status
- Region (Europe, North America, Pacific)
- Education Level

---

## Tools & Techniques Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts
- Slicers
- Data Categorization
- Data Visualization

---

## Key Insights
- Middle-aged customers show higher bike purchase rates
- Higher income groups are more likely to purchase bikes
- Customers with shorter commute distances tend to buy bikes more
- Education and region significantly influence purchase behavior
